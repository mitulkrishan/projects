Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for grilling

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Marinate the paneer cubes in the mixture for at least 1 hour.
