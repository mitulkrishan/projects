Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 bunch spinach (palak), blanched and pureed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add the pureed spinach and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in paneer cubes, garam masala, and salt. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
