Shahi Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, ground
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Almond slivers for garnish
