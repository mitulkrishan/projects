Maggi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves, chopped

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add water and bring it to a boil.
  4. Add Maggi noodles and the tastemaker from the packet. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala and chopped coriander leaves. Mix well.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi noodles!
