Egg Bhurji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, garam masala, and chili. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Crack the eggs directly into the pan, and scramble them until cooked.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with bread or roti.
