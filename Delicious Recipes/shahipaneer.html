Shahi Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, ground
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Almond slivers for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Cut 200g of paneer into cubes. Soak 10-12 cashew nuts in warm water for 10-15 minutes, then grind them into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil in a pan. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add 2 finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  6. Add the cashew nut paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, and salt to taste. Cook the mixture for a few more minutes until the oil starts to separate.
  8. Add the paneer cubes to the gravy and mix gently so that the paneer is well coated with the masala.
  9. Add 1/2 cup of fresh cream and 1/2 cup of milk. Stir well and cook for another 3-4 minutes on low heat.
  10. Add 1 teaspoon of garam masala and mix gently.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a few saffron strands soaked in a little milk.
  12. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
