Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan, and saut√© onions and vegetables until tender.
  2. Push the veggies to one side and scramble the egg (optional) on the other side.
  3. Add the rice, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
