Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and black pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and let them cook for a few seconds.
  4. Sprinkle chopped onions, tomatoes, and bell peppers over the eggs.
  5. Cook until the eggs are set, then fold the omelette in half.
  6. Optionally, sprinkle grated cheese before folding the omelette.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your omelette!
