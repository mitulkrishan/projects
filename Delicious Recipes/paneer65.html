Paneer 65 Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, ginger-garlic paste, and salt to make a marinade.
  2. Marinate the paneer cubes for 30 minutes.
  3. Deep fry the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown and crispy.
