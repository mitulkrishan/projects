Paneer 65 Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. Marinate the paneer cubes with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Mix cornflour and all-purpose flour with water to make a batter.
  3. Dip the marinated paneer in the batter and deep fry until golden brown.

  5. Add the fried paneer and toss well. Serve hot.
