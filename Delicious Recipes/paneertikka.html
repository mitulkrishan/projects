Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for grilling

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and mustard oil to make the marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  4. Thread the marinated paneer onto skewers.
  5. Bake the paneer skewers in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the paneer is golden brown, turning them halfway through.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
