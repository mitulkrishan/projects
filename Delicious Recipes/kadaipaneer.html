Kadai Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add sliced bell peppers and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
