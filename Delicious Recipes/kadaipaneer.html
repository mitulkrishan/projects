Kadai Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
