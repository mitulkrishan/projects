Upma Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup peas (optional)
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions, grated carrots, and peas. Saut√© until soft.
  3. Add turmeric powder and salt, then mix well.
  4. Add water to the pan and bring to a boil.
  5. Slowly add the semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cover the pan and let it cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot!
